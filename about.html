Our Story

Baked with heart, shared with love 🤎

A small kitchen, a big passion, and the belief that every bite should feel like a warm hug.

How it started

From weekend bakes to a brand

It started with a simple idea: what if every cookie was made with the same care you'd give to baking for someone you love? cookieesandcakes was born out of weekend experiments, a dog-eared recipe notebook, and an oven that never seemed to stop.

What began as gifts for friends and family quickly grew into something bigger — a community of people who believe that the best things in life are homemade, unhurried, and shared. Today, every order is still baked by hand in small batches, with the same love and attention as the very first one.

We source real, quality ingredients. We never freeze our bakes. We care deeply about how your order looks, tastes, and arrives. Because to us, baking isn't just a business — it's a form of love made edible.
