Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order? +

There's no minimum for cookies and brownies — you can order as few as 1! For custom cakes, our minimum order is a 6-inch cake. Gift boxes start at 12 pieces.

How much notice do you need for an order? +

We require at least 48 hours' notice for cookies and brownies, and 5–7 days for custom cakes. For large orders or event catering, please reach out at least 2 weeks in advance.

Do you offer delivery? +

Yes! We offer local delivery within a 15 km radius for a flat fee. Local pickup is always free. Delivery details and fees will be confirmed when we respond to your inquiry.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions? +

We can accommodate many dietary needs including gluten-free and nut-free options for select items. Please mention your requirements in the order form and we'll do our best to help. Note: our kitchen does handle common allergens.

How do I pay for my order? +

We currently accept payment via bank transfer, PayPal, or cash on pickup. A 50% deposit is required to confirm custom cake orders. Full payment details will be provided when we confirm your order.
