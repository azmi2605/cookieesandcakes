Handcrafted with Purpose

At cookieesandcakes, our journey began in a tiny home kitchen with nothing but a sourdough starter and a dream to bring back the sensory joy of slow baking.

We believe that every ingredient tells a story. That's why we source our flour from local heritage mills and our chocolate from ethical producers. No preservatives, no shortcuts—just pure, artisanal passion in every crumb.

  • 100% Organic, Stone-Ground Flour
  • Single-Origin Dark Chocolate
  • Small Batch Daily Production
