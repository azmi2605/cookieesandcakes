1. Overview

cookieesandcakes ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or place an order with us.

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy or our practices, please contact us using the details provided at the end of this document.

2. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and data gathered automatically during your use of our website.

person Personal Information You Provide

  • Full name and contact details (email, phone, address)
  • Payment information (processed securely — not stored by us)
  • Order history and preferences
  • Account credentials (email and hashed password)
  • Dietary preferences and allergen information (if voluntarily provided)

monitoring Automatically Collected Data

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited, time spent, and clickstream data
  • Device information (OS, screen resolution)
  • Referring URLs and search terms

4. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and understand site usage.

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Session management, cart, login state Session
Preference Language, region, display settings 1 year
Analytics Usage patterns and performance metrics 2 years
Marketing Personalised ads (opt-in only) 90 days

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

5. Sharing Your Data

sell

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties for their marketing purposes.

We may share your data only with:

  • Payment processors — to handle transactions securely (e.g., Stripe, PayPal).
  • Delivery partners — to fulfil and track your orders.
  • Analytics providers — for aggregated, anonymised usage insights.
  • Legal authorities — when required by law or court order.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit, hashed password storage, and access controls limiting who can view your data within our team.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

visibility Right to access your data
edit Right to correct inaccurate data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@cookieesandcakes.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete it.
